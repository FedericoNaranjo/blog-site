Cult Members Excited After Antichrist Elected President

 

When I possibly murder everyone that made fun of me, I'll have our presidentís approval. Devil curse America.

After the shocking results of the American presidential election, cult members everywhere are celebrating what many are considering the “Beginning of the End”. The anonymous head of the AADM (American Association of Death and Murder) released this statement hours after the election results became finalized. "For too long, we have been oppressed, ridiculed, and otherized. My daughter's friends won't stay over because their parents think we're weirdos. They'd always laugh, and make these little snide remarks about how just because we practice ritualistic sacrifice we don't belong in their version of society. But now, we don't have to put up with this pro-life privilege." Others have expressed similar sentiments online. "Bring on WW3, the people have spoken!", "Enough of all these ridiculous rules based around helping people and protecting life. Time to make America great again!”, "Glad we elected a president who will protect our constitutional right to bear arms, and use them to murder people who have different opinions than me." and "Looking forward to 8 years of greatness (or less if we all die before then)." While the president-elect has yet to publicly voice his support for the group who experiences perhaps more discrimination than any other in the world, one thing is for certain, his message has inspired hope for those who desperately needed it. "Every day I used to go to school with a sense of dread. But now I have hope, hope that one day, when I possibly murder everyone that made fun of me, I'll have our president’s approval. Devil curse America.
